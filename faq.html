Frequently asked questions
What is DeFiPatrol?

DeFiPatrol is a crypto portfolio tracking tool that allows users to track their decentralized finance (DeFi) assets and monitor their performance in real-time.

What are the features of DeFiPatrol?

DeFiPatrol provides a range of features to help users track their DeFi portfolio, including portfolio balance, price tracking, transaction history, and alerts for price changes. Additionally, it offers advanced analytics and visualizations to give users a deeper understanding of their portfolio's performance.

How does DeFiPatrol connect to my DeFi accounts?

DeFiPatrol connects to your DeFi accounts through API integration. This allows the tool to automatically import and update information about your assets.

How does DeFiPatrol ensure the security of my data?

DeFiPatrol employs advanced security measures to protect your data, including multifactor authentication and encryption of all data stored on our servers. However, no security measures are perfect, and we cannot guarantee the security of your personal information.

Can I connect multiple wallets and protocols to DeFiPatrol?

Yes, DeFiPatrol supports multiple integrations to allow users to connect multiple wallets and protocols to the tool.

How often are the prices updated in DeFiPatrol?

DeFiPatrol updates prices every 24 hours, or every time the user logs in to DefiPatrol, or when you click on the refresh button on the portfolio page so you always have the most current information about the performance of your crypto assets.

Can I set alerts for my assets?

Yes, DeFiPatrol allows you to set alerts for specific assets or portfolio value changes.

Can I track my NFTs on DeFiPatrol?

Yes, DeFiPatrol allows you to track your NFTs and see their performance in your portfolio.

How can I access my transaction history on DeFiPatrol?

DeFiPatrol allows you to view your transaction history in your account dashboard, where you can see all of your past transactions.

Is there a limit to the number of assets I can track on DeFiPatrol?

No, there is no limit to the number of assets you can track on DeFiPatrol.
